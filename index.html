Title: Doughnut
Edition: 1 of 54
Marketplace: Rarible
Blockchain: Ethereum

Collected: Jan 6th, 2022

✍️ Notes: I came across Susan Kare when reading about the inspiration for the Every Icon project. Susan is known as "the woman who gave the macintosh a smile".
